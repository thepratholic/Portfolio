I'm Pratham — a final-year Computer Science student at BVM Engineering College, Gujarat. My focus is on backend engineering: building systems that are fast, reliable, and battle-tested.

In-Memory Key-Value Cache

A Redis-inspired in-memory KV cache in Python with 16-shard architecture and a custom RWLock.

  • DJB2 hashing + bitmask for O(1) shard selection; 700K entry cap with auto-eviction
